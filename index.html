Frequently Asked Questions

What is the return Policy?
Thank you for your purchase. We hope you are happy with your purchase. However, if you are not completely satisfied with your purchase for any reason, you may return it to us for [a full refund], [store credit], or [an exchange].
When will i receive my order?
Our delivery times are estimates based on your delivery address. Special orders, out-of-stock products and items on back order can all increase delivery times. (This applies particularly to spare parts and suppliers based outside of Denmark.) You can always check on the status of your order by using the link you received after you placed the order. You will receive an email when your package leaves our warehouse. This mail will contain a tracking number that you can use to trace your package online.
Where are you located?
Starbucks places its coffee houses in high-street, high-visibility locations in various settings, including downtown and suburban areas.
